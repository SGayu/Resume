5S    

FEBRUARY 27,2025

5S is a workplace organization method that improves efficiency through five steps:

1.Sort :  Remove unnecessary items.

 

2.Set in Order :  Organize remaining items.

3.Shine : Clean and maintain the workplace.

4.Standardize : Create consistent practices.

5.Sustain : Make 5S habits part of the culture.

It helps reduce waste and improve productivity.
